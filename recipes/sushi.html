Maki Roll

Description

Maki is just nori wrapped around rice with a filling in the absolute centre. Often seafood but can be filled with anything. Most commonly salmon, crab, avo, or tuna

Traditional Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Nori
  • Sushi Rice
  • Soya Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook Rice
  2. Slice Tuna
  3. Lay Nori
  4. Add rice on Nori
  5. Add Tuna
  6. Roll into cylinder, and cut into 8 pieces
